Adhyaya 1.1: fill 29 missing sutras with discrete Panini predicates

Add sutra_impl_1_1.py covering the 29 Adhyaya-1.1 sutras that were
absent from the inline registry. Each predicate tests the specific
Pāṇinian condition the sūtra names; each carries a positive fixture
that fires it and a near-miss negative that rejects it.
